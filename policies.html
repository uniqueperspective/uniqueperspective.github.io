Shipping Policy

We ship all orders within 1–3 business days. Free shipping is available on orders over $75. Delivery times vary by location, typically 3–7 business days for domestic orders.

Return & Refund Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original condition. Custom jewelry orders are final sale unless there is a defect. To initiate a return, contact us at payments4uniqueperspective@gmail.com.

Homemade Jewelry Note

All handmade jewelry is one-of-a-kind. Slight variations in color, size, and finish are part of the charm and are not considered defects. Product photos may vary slightly from the finished piece due to natural material variation.
