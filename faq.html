FAQ

Common questions, honest answers

Where do your stones come from?

All of our stones are hand-mined from small, ethical mining operations. We know where every stone comes from and ensure that our sourcing supports local communities rather than industrial extraction.

Are your jewelry pieces really handmade?

Yes! Every handcrafted piece in our collection is made by hand with care and intention. We also carry curated dropshipping items — these are clearly labeled in the shop.

Do you accept custom orders?

Absolutely. We love custom work. If you have a specific stone, design, or vision in mind, reach out via our contact page and we'll be happy to discuss options.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in their original condition and packaging. See our full Refund & Returns Policy for details.

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 2–5 business days. Shipping times vary by location. Custom orders may take longer — we'll always give you an estimated timeline upfront.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs fees and duties depending on your country — these are the buyer's responsibility.

What stones do you typically carry?

Our collection changes based on what we mine and source, but commonly includes pyrite, jasper, quartz, amethyst, obsidian, and more. Check the shop regularly for new arrivals.


Still have questions? Contact us — we're happy to help.
