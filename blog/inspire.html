Inspire

December 30, 2025

  1. Experimental Typography Projects • “Typographic Experiments” by Erik Spiekermann A master class in mixing legibility with artful distortion and innovation. • Daniel Britton’s Kinetic Typography Dynamic motion typography projects that combine storytelling with fluid type animation.
    1. 3D & Isometric Typography
      • Louise Fili’s Vintage-Inspired Designs Classic meets dimensional type with a timeless yet modern twist.
      • Isometric Type by Dribbble Artists Search “isometric typography” on Dribbble for amazing examples combining 3D grids and type.
    2. Mixed Media Typography
      • Jessica Walsh’s Work at &Walsh Bold use of colors, textures, and mixed media to create vibrant type-based designs.
      • Collage Typography by Seb Lester Hand-lettering meets collage art in striking visual compositions.
    3. Negative Space & Optical Illusions
      • Noma Bar’s Negative Space Illustrations Clever use of space within letters to tell dual stories or hidden meanings.
      • Optical Illusion Fonts by Typotheque Fonts designed to play with perception and depth.
