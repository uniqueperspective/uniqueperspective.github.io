December 30, 2025

Pyrite Crystals

About Pyrite

Pyrite, often called "Fool's Gold" due to its metallic golden appearance, is an iron sulfide mineral known for its protective and manifestation properties. Its name comes from the Greek word "pyr," meaning fire, as it creates sparks when struck against metal or stone. This powerful stone has been valued throughout history for both its striking appearance and its metaphysical properties.
Chemical Composition
Iron Sulfide (FeS₂)
Color
Metallic gold, brassy yellow
Hardness
6-6.5 on Mohs scale
Origin
Peru, Spain, Italy, USA, Russia
